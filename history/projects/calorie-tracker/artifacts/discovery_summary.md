# Discovery Summary: Calorie Tracking App

## Summary
User wants to build a calorie tracking application. Input is high-level; details need discovery.

## Key User Problems (Inferred)
1. Difficulty tracking daily food intake accurately
2. Lack of awareness of caloric content in meals
3. No easy way to maintain consistent eating habits
4. Manual logging is tedious and error-prone
5. Hard to connect food choices with health goals

## Target Users (Assumed)
- Health-conscious individuals wanting to manage weight
- Fitness enthusiasts tracking macros
- People with dietary restrictions or health conditions
- Users new to calorie counting seeking simple tools

## Goals & Desired Outcomes (Inferred)
- Easy food logging (minimal friction)
- Accurate calorie data
- Progress visibility over time
- Habit formation support

## Assumptions
- Mobile-first experience (most calorie tracking happens on-the-go)
- Food database or API integration needed
- User accounts required for data persistence
- Basic reporting/analytics expected

## Risks
- Competitive market (MyFitnessPal, Lose It!, etc.)
- Food database accuracy and coverage
- User retention (logging fatigue is common)
